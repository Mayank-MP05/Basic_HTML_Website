Solarium : Team Solarium is a group of engineering students from the Pimpri Chinchwad College Of Engineering who design and manufacture an electric-solar vehicle. We aspire to innovate in the field of solar energy and take our racing innovations to a commercial application.



The team was formed in the month of August 2016 under the guidance of our esteemed teachers and advisers Dr. Anindita Roy, Mr. Pankaj Anjankar & Mr. Ganesh Kale.  The team then started its designs and buckled up with great dedication and passion to manufacture Invictus v1.0.



Invictus v1.0 was designed and manufactured as per rules and guidelines of National Solar Vehicle Challenge a national level event. The vehicle had various innovations added to it which were never used in any other racing or commercial system. Our innovations and unaltered manufacturing helped us bag various awards at the same right from best design award to autocross.

Each year Team Solarium designs a passenger vehicle based on the last season's racing vehicle which can be used for travels, transportation and leisure rides.



Now the team is in process for designing and manufacturing Invictus v2.0 which will have new innovative components and one of it's kind unique solar electric racing vehicle in India.

Reb BAron : Team Red Baron is a group of undergraduate students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, Pune.
